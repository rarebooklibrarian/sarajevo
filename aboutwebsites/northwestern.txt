2019-21 Strategic Plan: Bridging to 2022
Library Organizational Model 
The goals of Northwestern University Libraries are outlined in the 2019-21 Strategic Plan: Bridging to 2022.
Northwestern University Libraries deliver a suite of state-of-the-art information and research services across multiple physical and digital locations. We customize four major sets of academic resources – collections, technologies, professional expertise, and specialized facilities – to advance the university’s academic goals:

Enhance the University’s Intellectual Eminence and Impact
Continue to Build Research Eminence
Support the Student Experience
Enhance Global Academic Reputation and Presence
Strengthen the Enabling Support for Our Ambitions
